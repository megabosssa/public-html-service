Privacy Policy

Black Cat Watch Face

Last updated: 2025-07-14

1. Overview

Black Cat Watch Face (“the App”) is a Wear OS watch-face application that displays time, date, and user-selected complications on your smartwatch. We do not collect, store, process, or share any personal or sensitive data from users or their devices.

2. Data Collection & Usage

CategoryDetails
Personal dataNone collected
Sensitive data (health, location, contacts, etc.)None collected
Device identifiers & analyticsNone collected
On-device info (battery %, steps, etc.)Displayed locally via Wear OS complications; never transmitted or stored outside your watch

3. Permissions

The App does not request any runtime permissions. It relies solely on the standard Wear OS complication API to show information you explicitly select.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. The App contains no advertising or analytics SDKs.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is directed at a general audience and does not target children under 13. Since no data is collected, the App complies with COPPA and similar child-privacy regulations.

6. Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach originating from this App.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we add features requiring data collection, this Privacy Policy will be updated and users will be notified through the Play Store listing before the changes take effect.
